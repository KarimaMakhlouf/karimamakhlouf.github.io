About Me

I am an instructor in the Department of Data Science and Artificial Intelligence at the University of Doha for Science and Technology (UDST), Doha, Qatar.

My research focuses on ethical and trustworthy artificial intelligence, particularly privacy and fairness in large language models.

Research Interests

  • Trustworthy AI and Responsible AI Deployment
  • Privacy Risks in Large Language Models
  • Privacy-Preserving Technologies
  • Causality from a Fairness Perspective
