About Me

I am an instructor in the Department of Data Science and Artificial Intelligence at the University of Doha for Science and Technology.

My research focuses on ethical and trustworthy artificial intelligence, particularly privacy and fairness in large language models.

Research Interests

  • Trustworthy artificial intelligence
  • Privacy risks in large language models
  • Algorithmic fairness evaluation
  • Responsible AI deployment
  • AI and cybersecurity

Teaching

  • Data science

  • Parallel and distributed computing
